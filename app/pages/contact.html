How to get here

In the end, you’ll have to do the very last bit
like they’ve done it for centuries: On foot.
Because the old city centre is a car-free zone.

By Taxi

Please instruct the taxi driver to drop you off at the Breestraat bus stop. Then take a short, stunning walk through the Maarsmansteeg to CTY Books. You can already see the hotel at the end of the narrow alley!

By Train and Bus

Arriving by train at Leiden Central Station, catch any of these buses to the Breestraat bus stop:
2/4/45/169/182/187/400/800.
Then take the same short hike through the narrow Maarsmansteeg to CTY Books.

How does the self check-in work?

Parking in the old town of Leiden can be challenging, especially for people who are not familiar with the area. The streets are narrow, the spots are sparse and the prices are steep. Plus: after parking your car on the street, you’d still have to walk a fair bit to our hotel, which is located in a car-free zone.

We recommend to park your car at the Haagweg parking lot. It is located just outside the city centre, at Haagweg 8, 2312 AA, Leiden. There is a free shuttle bus to drive you to the hotel.

Staying in touch during your stay

If you arrive to CTY Books ahead of check-in time, you can leave your luggage in one of the lockers, using your check-in code on your smartphone. Once it’s time to check in, you can use the same code to enter your room. In your room you will find a SuitePad that enables you to contact the concierge at all times of the day.
