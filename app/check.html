Visiting us by car?

Parking in the old town of Leiden can be challenging, especially for people who are not familiar with the area. The streets are narrow, the spots are sparse and the prices are steep. Plus: after parking your car on the street, you’d still have to walk a fair bit to our hotel, which is located in a car-free zone.

We recommend to park your car at the Haagweg parking lot. It is located just outside the city centre, at Haagweg 8, 2312 AA, Leiden. There is a free shuttle bus to drive you to the hotel.
